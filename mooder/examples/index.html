About

Why i do this?

We presented Mooder, a mood filter website that treat people with stress and help them feel better about themselves. This project targets mental health and provide an entertaining filter platform for people who experience stress.

How to use it?

Each button represents different filter feature. Down below is the instruction of how to use the filters.

Filter features <3

MOOD filter

Detecting your current mood based on current facial expression.

SAD filter

Many people mentioned that when they feel sad the encouragement from others can make them feel better. For this reason, I created this sad filter to encourage people and cheer them up whenever they are feeling down.

ANXIOUS filter

I create this filter to help people deal with stress, anxiety, and negative emotions. I made this breathing ball animation to help them cool down when their temper flares and sharpen their concentration skills. This filter can be utilized as a helper for breathing meditation or as many other meditative implements.
