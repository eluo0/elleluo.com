Scentery: A Calming Multisensory Environment By Mixing Virtual Reality, Sound, and scent

Author: Elle Luo, Katia Vega

Video production: Christine, Chong, Elle Luo

As stress levels continue to increase throughout the general population and the ability to find coping mechanisms, it becomes more essential to reach a healthy mental condition. A calming environment could psychologically reduce stress levels and increase an emotional sense of engagement. Scentery proposes a novel approach to create calming multisensory environments by displaying virtual reality, producing sound, and activating olfactory sensations. Emotive Design Taxonomy intersects emotions with colors, sounds and scents and the combinations of these factors are designed potentially for creating a calming multisensory environment.
